7) Very short viva answers you can memorize
DTW
DTW means Dynamic Time Warping. It compares two audio sequences even if one is recited faster or slower, so it is useful for matching user recitation with the reference qari.
Faster-Whisper
Faster-Whisper is the speech-to-text model we use to transcribe the user’s Arabic recitation into text before comparison.
Phoneme
A phoneme is the smallest sound unit of a language. We use phoneme-level comparison to check pronunciation accuracy.
Tajweed
Tajweed is the set of rules for correct Quran recitation, including pronunciation, elongation, nasalization, stopping, and merging rules.
Number of Tajweed rules
Our project detects 9 main Tajweed rule families, and some of them are further divided into detailed sub-rules.
